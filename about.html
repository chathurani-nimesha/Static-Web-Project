Golden Grand Hotel

Welcome to Golden Grand Hotel

Located right in the heart of No. 64, Lotus Road, Colombo 01, Sri Lanka, Golden Grand Hotel is your luxurious retreat that always promises to be an unforgettable experience. From business travelers to leisure guests, our elegant rooms, world-class amenities, and outstanding service afford our guests the perfect retreat. An aura of refinement and comfort will greet you from the very entrance through the beautifully designed lobby to inside.

Who We Are

Founded in 1990, Grand Hotel began as a small boutique retreat with a vision to offer unmatched hospitality in the city. Over the years, we have expanded and evolved into the luxurious destination we are today, all while maintaining the intimate and personalized service that has made us a favorite among travelers. Our heritage and commitment to excellence continue to drive us as we welcome guests from all corners of the globe.

At Golden Grand Hotel, our mission is simple: to provide every guest with a welcoming atmosphere, personalized attention, and exceptional service that turns moments into lasting memories. We believe that true hospitality means anticipating the needs of our guests, offering an unparalleled experience in every aspect of their stay. Our core values of integrity, passion for service, and commitment to sustainability are reflected in everything we do.

Our Team

At Golden Grand Hotel, our team is at the heart of everything we do. Committed to providing personalized and exceptional service, each member brings a unique blend of professionalism, passion, and hospitality expertise. From our friendly front desk staff to our dedicated housekeeping team, our mission is to ensure every guest feels welcomed and cared for from the moment they step through our doors. Led by seasoned hospitality professionals, we work together to create memorable experiences that exceed expectations and keep our guests coming back.
